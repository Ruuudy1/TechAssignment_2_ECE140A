Product Thinking Vocabulary

  1. Customer need: The product needs to either solve a issue the customer has or improve on a soluton that is already out there (e.g make it cheaper)
  2. Management: Managing not only your product but the market as a whole around it and the people it appeals to
  3. Problem Solving: encouraging creative thinking to solve a problem in an unconventional, out of the box approach
